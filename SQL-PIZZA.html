Unveiling Insights: Pizza sales Data Analysis

Pizza sales - Data Exploration

Introduction:

Data exploitation refers to the process of extracting valuable insights from raw data by applying analytical techniques. In the context of pizza sales, this can involve querying the sales database using SQL (Structured Query Language) to identify patterns, trends, and key metrics that can drive business decisions.

Goal:

Pizza sales data may include information such as order dates, customer demographics, pizza types, quantities, sales amounts, and more. Through SQL, businesses can harness this data for various purposes, including:

Outcome:

Performance Monitoring: Tracking total sales, peak sales periods, and overall revenue.
Customer Preferences: Understanding which pizza types are most popular among customers.
Sales Trends: Analyzing daily, weekly, or monthly sales trends to optimize inventory and marketing.
Profitability Insights: Identifying top-selling and low-performing pizzas to focus on high-profit items.

2.Highest-priced pizza:

  • This SQL query retrieves the name and price of the highest-priced pizza from the database.
    JOIN clause: The query combines data from two tables (pizza_types and pizzas) based on the pizza_type_id column that links the two tables.
    ORDER BY pizzas.price DESC: This sorts the pizzas in descending order by price, meaning the highest-priced pizza will appear first.
    LIMIT 1: Ensures that only the highest-priced pizza is returned.

  • Result: The result grid at the bottom shows the name of the highest-priced pizza:
    "The Greek Pizza" with a price of $35.95.
