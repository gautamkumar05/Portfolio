Elevating E-Commerce Insights: Madhav Sales Dashboard

E-commerce Sales Dashboard

Introduction:

In this project, the objective was to empower Madhav Store in tracking and analyzing their online sales across India through an interactive Power BI dashboard. The goal was to provide actionable insights for strategic decision-making. The outcome is a visually appealing and user-friendly dashboard that illuminates key metrics and trends in sales performance.

Goal:

The primary goal was to create a dynamic and interactive sales dashboard for Madhav Store, allowing for the comprehensive tracking and analysis of online sales. The project aimed to highlight top-performing states, customers, and categories, providing valuable insights for optimizing sales strategies.

Outcome:

The Madhav Sales Dashboard successfully uncovered crucial insights, such as Maharashtra leading in sales with 40.79% of the total amount, Harivansh being the top customer contributing 29.67% of the total amount, and Clothing dominating with 62.62% of the total quantity.

The dashboard showcases impactful visualizations and key metrics, offering a clear understanding of online sales dynamics and providing actionable insights for business growth.
